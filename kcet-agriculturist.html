Agriculture Quota (Farmers' Quota) ‚Äì KCET Admissions

50% of total seats reserved for students from agricultural backgrounds

Eligibility Criteria

Family Background:
  • Parents must be engaged in agricultural activities
  • Family should own at least 1 acre of agricultural land
  • Primary income source should be farming
Document Requirements:
  • KCET Admission Ticket
  • Agriculture Certificate from revenue department
  • Family tree form the Revenue Department
  • Family member agricultral certificate(if the agricultral land belong to the parents ancestors)
  • Income Certificate prooving that farming as main incomem source of that family
  • Affidavit - 1(As per the University forms)
  • Salary Certificate for the salaried class

Practical Examination

Conducted by:

  • KVAFSU, Bidar (Karnataka Veterinary, Animal and Fisheries Sciences University)
  • UAHS, Shivamogga (University of Agricultural and Horticultural Sciences)
  • UAS, Raichuru (University of Agricultural Sciences)
  • UAS, Dharawada (University of Agricultural Sciences)
  • UAS, Bengaluru (University of Agricultural Sciences)
  • UHS, Bhagalakote (University of Horticultural Sciences)

The practical examination is conducted region-wise, following the rules and regulations set by the university. It is held in accordance with the Rural Agriculture Dependency criteria, and the exams are conducted by university-affiliated Farm Science and Veterinary¬†Colleges.
